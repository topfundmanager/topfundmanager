My Story & Why Im Qualified to Coach You:
I am an Ivy League Graduate of Cornell University in Ithaca, NY. I graduated early with a degree in Economics top of my class. After my departure from Cornell, I went to start my own real estate investing business at the age of 21.
I eventually went on to become a self-made multi-millionaire at the age of 28. However, I had to pull myself out of financial ruin 1st. The market crash of 2008 left me with horrible credit, no income, a foreclosure and a pending bankruptcy! During that hard period, I never gave up on myself and my passion for real estate. I eventually rose to the occasion and developed amazing recession proof strategies that made me a millionaire in the worst real estate market in a generation. I did all this by learning how to make money out of nothing! Yes, I said nothing!
My hardships and failures taught me invaluable lessons you will learn in this course. Now I am a bestselling author, have acquired over 400 deals, currently have an 8-figure real estate portfolio and did all this by using strategy you can do yourself!
I have helped 1000s of people achieve financial freedom using my real estate investment strategies. Youre up next!
